COMMUNICATIONS AND MARKETING CONTRACTOR

About Callisto:

Callisto is a rapidly growing nonprofit tech startup that has created an online sexual assault reporting and documentation system that helps identify serial sexual predators. The system launched 2.5 years ago on 2 pilot college campuses, and has since grown to 12 colleges and an entertainment industry pilot. Reporting on participating campuses has doubled, assaults have been reported 60% faster, and 15% of victims have matched with another victim of the same assailant (meaning they were informed that they weren't the only one and connected to the same investigator or legal advisor). In the next year, we will be rapidly expanding to address sexual assault and coercion in multiple industries, connecting victims of the same predator together and with legal support.

About The Position:

Callisto is seeking a 3-month Communications and Marketing Contractor, with possibility for extension of a full-time employment offer. Callisto is a nonprofit startup that creates technology to combat sexual assault and harassment. Our primary initiative is an online sexual assault documentation and reporting system, which began on college campuses and is now expanding to the entertainment industry.

We’re hiring a contractor to provide communications and marketing to support fundraising, demand creation, impact, and brand awareness. The contractor will drive toward these goals by leveraging social and traditional media and owned content to help Callisto consistently articulate its mission to a range of stakeholders. We prefer the contractor to be located in San Francisco, but will consider remote applicants.

The contractor will work closely with members of the Callisto Executive Team and other peers within the organization on variety of strategic communications and marketing needs.

Responsibilities:
  • •Overseeing day-to-day social media activity, including posting on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram and managing paid advertising
  • •Managing reactive and proactive media relations and tracking coverage
  • •Drafting and sending monthly emails via Mailchimp
  • •Developing talking points and messaging frameworks
  • •Ensuring quality and branding of all externally-facing communication
  • •Managing development, distribution, and maintenance of all print and digital collateral including, but not limited to, e-newsletters, press releases, on-campus educational materials, and the Callisto marketing website
  • •Managing external contractors, such as advertising/marketing firms, videographers, graphic designers, etc.
You Have:
  • •4+ years of communications and marketing experience, ideally in a startup or small nonprofit setting
  • •Excellent writing/editing and verbal communication skills
  • •An eye for design
  • •Ability to work independently with minimal guidance
Bonus Points For:
  • •Graphic design experience
  • •Experience working with remote teams
