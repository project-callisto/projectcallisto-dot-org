Partner With Us

For students, Callisto offers options so they can make the reporting choices that are best for them. For administrators, our system provides the data necessary to improve policies to respond to, and prevent, sexual assault.

By becoming a Callisto Campus, you are showing a commitment to your students, survivors, and the movement to end sexual assault.

The Impact

Low reporting rates and delayed reports that lack detail and evidence make it extremely difficult for campuses to investigate sexual assault and hold perpetrators accountable.

Callisto has shown to increase reporting and facilitate more rapid and thorough investigations. On one campus, Callisto helped to quadruple the reporting rate.

Callisto provides its partners with a securely hosted website that includes customized content, assessment and evaluation, materials to engage the campus community, and active partner support. Hear what our partners had to say.
