Where Your Gift Goes

When you give to Callisto you're not just donating to an organization, you're helping sexual assault survivors heal, gain support, and obtain justice in a way that is accessible and confidential. You're investing in a more empowering reporting experience for survivors, the collection of better evidence and data on sexual assault, and the ability to identify and stop repeat perpetrators.

Our Supporters

Callisto has an incredible community of donors and volunteers whose contributions escalate our impact and make our work possible. Our funders include Gooogle.org, The New York State Health Foundation, The New York Women's Foundation, The Levi Strauss Foundation, NoVo Foundation, The Horace W. Goldsmith Foundation, PIMCO Foundation, Fast Forward, The Harnisch Foundation, and many other amazing foundation and corporate supporters. We are also supported by over 600 individual donors who contribute to our work. Many are survivors or allies of survivors who care deeply about Callisto and the impact it has on the lives of those who have experienced sexual assault. We are grateful to each and every one of them.
