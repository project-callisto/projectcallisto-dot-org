We're still here, and we're still fighting for sexual assault survivors.

We know how vital it is at this time to support survivors and maintain the momentum of our movement. Callisto will continue to center and uplift the voices of survivors in the months and years to come. Join us in our work by contributing a gift to Callisto today.

Gifts of $100 or more made by December 31, 2016 will be matched by a generous anonymous donor.
Give Now

Where Your Gift Goes

Your contribution will directly assist Callisto in our pursuit of a world where survivors are supported and sexual assault is rare and unacceptable. It will benefit our work to build technology that creates a more empowering reporting experience for survivors, provides authorities with better evidence and data on sexual assault, and facilitates the identification of repeat perpetrators.

A Matching Pledge for New Donors

An anonymous donor has pledged to match all contributions made by new donors between $100 and $999 1:1 and gifts between $1,000 and $2,500 2:1. All donations must be made by December 31, 2016. A new donor is any individual who has not made a contribution to Callisto after January 1, 2015.
