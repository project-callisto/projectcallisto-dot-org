Who We Are

We are engineers, designers, legal experts, researchers, and educators—united in our desire to create a world where sexual assault is rare and survivors are supported.

join our team

Our Mission & Vision

Our vision is a world where sexual assault is rare and survivors are supported. Our mission is to create technology that combats sexual assault, supports survivors, and advances justice.
