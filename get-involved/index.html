Become a Callisto Campus Partner

Our vision is to enable survivors to find support and identify repeat perpetrators, wherever they are. Help take action to bring Callisto to a campus, institution, or professional setting near you.

By becoming a Callisto Campus, you are showing a commitment to your students, survivors, and the movement to end sexual assault. If you are a school administrator, request a demo.

If you are a student, use our mobilizing for Callisto toolkit to learn how you can bring Callisto to your school. You can also suggest a school using the form below.
