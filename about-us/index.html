Our History

In November 2011, Callisto CEO Jess Ladd founded Sexual Health Innovations (SHI) while she was a graduate student at Johns Hopkins University. SHI was founded with the goal of creating technology to advance sexual health and wellbeing in the United States. SHI’s initiatives were intended to solve sexual health problems on a structural basis—yielding truly disruptive solutions that positively impact stakeholders not just at the individual level, but at the community level.

After creating two technologies focused on sexually transmitted infection partner notification and test result delivery, SHI pivoted our focus to provide tech-based solutions for sexual assault survivor support and reporting. In 2014, we began formative research and fundraising for Callisto and announced the initiative at The White House Data Jam on Campus Sexual Assault.

A year-long pilot of Callisto launched in August 2015 at the University of San Francisco and Pomona College. Callisto’s successful pilot, growing demand for the system both within and outside of the college setting, and a growing national dialogue on campus sexual violence led SHI to decide in the summer of 2016 to reorganize and rebrand as Callisto.

Today, Callisto is available at a growing number of colleges nationwide and we’re beginning exploration of new opportunities for Callisto’s application within other community settings.

Senior Director of Communications & Marketing

Callisto is offering a unique opportunity to join our growing team as our Senior Director of Communications & Marketing. Callisto is a nonprofit startup that creates technology to combat sexual assault and support survivors in the United States. Our primary initiative is an online sexual assault documentation and reporting system.

We’re hiring a Senior Director of Communications & Marketing to set and guide the strategy for all communications, organizational website, and public relations messages and collateral to consistently articulate Callisto’s mission to a range of stakeholders. The Senior Director of Communications & Marketing will be located in either New York City or San Francisco.

The Senior Director of Communications & Marketing will work closely with members of the Callisto Executive Team and other peers within the organization on variety of strategic communications and marketing needs.

These responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Development, implementation, and evaluation of the annual communications plans across audiences in collaboration with relevant department staff
  • Generate digital content and create an online content strategy that engages audience segments and leads to measurable action
  • Put communications and marketing vehicles (including day-to-day social media) in place to create momentum and awareness for Callisto’s work as well as to test the effectiveness of communications activities
  • Ensure all externally-facing communication is on-brand and on-message
  • Manage development, distribution, and maintenance of all print and digital collateral including, but not limited to, e-newsletters, press releases, on-campus educational materials, and the Callisto marketing website
  • Manage media contacts and press hits
  • Support evaluation of potential media appearances for Callisto Executive Team members
  • Identify, recruit, and manage external contractors, such as marketing firms, videographers, graphic designers, etc.

You have:

  • 4+ years of communications and marketing experience, ideally in a startup or small nonprofit setting
  • Excellent writing/editing and verbal communication skills
  • An eye for design (you may not be a designer yourself, but you can evaluate whether materials are on-brand)
  • Ability to work collaboratively across departments to achieve communications marketing goals
  • A strong track record of being able to both build and implement communications and marketing strategies
  • Ability to work independently with minimal guidance

Bonus points for:

  • Graphic design experience
  • Experience working with remote teams

We'll give you:

  • Flexible work hours
  • Health, vision and dental insurance
  • Paid holidays, vacation, and sick leave
  • Salary commensurate with experience
  • A professional development budget and guidance to help your career grow as our organization grows
